The Stori Dori is a new luxurious naturally cured ‘veg tan’ travellers notebook. By incorporating an ingeniously simple method of binding your interchangeable inserts; your organiser becomes your journal, your planner, your creative spaaaaaace, or all of the above. Available in many sizes and colours at www.storidori.com

 

Islamic Planner Series® Planner & Agenda for Muslim family: With the ultimate mission to encourage people to be more focused in their religious affairs, Kecilmamil’s design is emphasized to create the details of day-to-day ibadaah as well as introducing again the values of Sunnah in each of her planner. With this, she has also produced another lineup in the series such as “The Academic Planner” for Muslim students since 2014 and “The Ramadan Planner 2016” in digital version for the first time through Etsy. www.etsy.com/shop/Kecilmamil

 

Samar Asamoah aka Yezarck’s books are completely handmade with love and care. She really enjoys making books for herself to use for sketching and drawing and has made some available to the public. They are all unique and individual pieces of art. Available through her Etsy shop: https://www.etsy.com/shop/Yezarck

 

Umm Leenah, (together with her headband-toting toddler), runs the site TheOrganizedMuslimah.Com where she offers her own unique, simple yet stunning planners to help Muslimahs remain organized and productive in not just their Dunya related chores, but also their Islamic activities such as planning their weekly Qur’an recitation, their sadaqah/good deeds and keeping track of their prayers. An all-in-one solution alhamdulillah! Visit The Vault to find downloadable planners – www.theorganizedmuslimah.com/thevault/

 

The Opitmuslim! This little planner journal is designed to be your “go-to” PJ (Planner Journal). It’s as easy or as complex as you need it to be. It will come with a basic kit and an exclusive collection of planner helpfuls and pretties, which are add-ons you can purchase in various formats, such as ready made, custom made, kits or digital designs. It’s a super convenient 3″ x 5″ size to drop right in your purse, diaper bag, back pack or even your pocket. Available Feb 2nd 2106 via www.domesticraft.com or go here for a sneak peek at the prototype that started the Optimuslim Planner journey – http://bit.ly/1uSTjwT

 

Hafsa Khizer of “The Reminder Series” aims to create journals that inspire having private conversations with Allah throughout your day. Tiny spiritual companions with your own collection of uplifting verses, duas, conversations, and reflections. The “My Alhamdullilah journal”, “Quranic Ayahs that me Happy”, “Dear Allah” and The “Iman Boosting Reflections” were created to inspire dhikr. Insha – allah! www.hafsakhizer.com or www.TheReminderSeries.etsy.com

The Clarity Journal is meant to encourage you to organize your thoughts by storing the good ones and throwing away the bad ones. The best way to see the picture clearly in any aspect of life – school, work, relationships and oneself, is to reflect. Focus on the virtues and make peace with the flaws. A portion from each sale of Clarity will be donated to a deserving charity in the Greater Toronto Area. To purchase your very own copy of the clarity journal, email ink.tweed@gmail.com or visit www.facebook.com/clarityjournal

 

Living Sunnah Rabi al Awwal and Ramadan Battle Plan Planners by Halalify: “When I took shahada in 2006 I naturally created a planner to facilitate my transition. At the time I was a new Muslim trying to build good habits like praying salat on time so I started scheduling them in so I wouldn’t forget. After befriending other converts I noticed a recurring theme of wishing to get organized and on point with ibaadah but unsure on how to get started. I kept tinkering with it and my flagship planner Ramadan Battle Plan emerged and quickly became popular, alhamdulillah. My complimentary digital version is my waqf to the ummah. InshaAllah ;)” To find her planners and downloads go to http://halalify.com
