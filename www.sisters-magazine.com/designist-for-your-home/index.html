Cactus Dryer buddies – EUR 8.50

These little fellas can help you save money and energy, and they’re super cute! Pop the cacti in your tumble dryer so they can disperse the wet clothes and promote efficient circulation of heat and removal of moisture. The spikes on the cacti gentle pamper the fabrics as they tumble, making them fluffier and reducing static. A great way to control dryer static and to naturally soften your clothes.

 

 

 

Batter Shaker – EUR 26

A deadly product that means you can have pancakes any time, any day, in no time. The jar comes with two silicone balls and a mesh lid, so when you shake it, it will whisk your batter in a jiffy. AND, you don’t even have to measure…there are markings on the side showing you how much of each ingredient you need to use to make pancakes or crepes. Genius! You can use the blender for preparing any liquid or foamy batters or to whip cream in a fast and fun way. Made of BPA free plastic and platinum silicon material which is easy to clean and repels water and germs. It’s a highly hygienic and dishwasher friendly material.

 

 

 

 

Bread Maker – EUR 30

 

Have you ever wanted to make your own bread and thought ‘it’s just too much hassle’? Well, with this awesome kit from Lekue it couldn’t be easier. With the Bread Maker, you can weigh, knead and bake all in the one receptacle. So now you can bake bread the traditional way, except it will be faster, cleaner and take up less space in the kitchen. The bowl is designed so it’s partially closed when you’re baking, allowing steam to circulate, which makes the dough moist and prevents your bread from drying out. The side openings allow the hot air to circulate, which gives your bread a crusty and golden texture. Made from platinum silicone, it’s designed to withstand high temperatures in ovens microwaves and dishwashers, and it can be reused as many times as needed. The kits comes with a dishwasher safe spatula, instruction booklet and recipes for 6-Grain bread and Quick No-Knead Bread. Made from platinum silicon material which is easy to clean and repels water and germs. It’s a highly hygienic and dishwasher friendly material.

 

 

 

 

Fermentation Crock – EUR 39

 

Fermentation is all the rage these days, but what exactly is it? It’s a way of preserving fresh vegetables whilst increasing their nutritional content at the same time. The action of the bacteria created during fermentation helps the body absorb the minerals in the food faster. The bacteria also produce vitamins and enzymes that are beneficial for digestion. You don’t need much equipment-wise to ferment your own vegetables, but having the correct crock pot can make all the difference when you’re getting started. This crock pot provides a special anaerobic (without air) fermenting environment. It allows the lactic-acid bacteria to work best and prevents yeasts and moulds which can ruin your ferment and make your food taste gross. This 2 litre kit has everything you need to start making your own pickles at home: a durable, cleverly designed fermentation crock, a re-writable label to identify each batch, and a booklet of user-friendly, thoroughly tested recipes to get you started.

 

 

 

 

Eden Suction Planter – EUR 17.50

 

The Eden Suction Planter by Okidome is a new concept in indoor gardening; plant pot that attaches to windows, tiles and the fridge. So your plants get way more light, and they are in sight at all times, absolute genius. Ideal for herbs in the kitchen, they are easy to install and easy to remove. You really need to use it to believe it, but they come with a secure and powerful suction (the Oki-Lock system) and it also incorporates a water reservoir system.

 

 

 

Popcorn Maker – EUR 21.50

 

Prepare healthy and original home-made popcorn in just 2-3 minutes in the microwave with this very handy platinum silcone bowl. Use the measurements marked on the bowl to pour out 50g or 100g of corn, add a tablespoon of oil and salt to taste, pop the lid on loosely, and microwave for 2-5 mins. Easy peasy! And for storage, the bowl folds from 14.5 cm down to 6 cm high! The box comes with some delicious recipes for popcorn; try Bollywood Movie style with curry, Fantasy Movie with butter and herbs or Adventure Movie with nuts and honey. Yummers! Made of platinum silicon material which is easy to clean and repels water and germs. It’s a highly hygienic, BPA free and dishwasher friendly material.

 

 

 

Sugru – EUR 10

 

This really is wonderstuff! Sugru is like modeling clay when you take it from its pack. Once it’s exposed to air, it cures to a tough flexible silicone overnight using the moisture in the air. Working time = 30 mins. Cure time = 24hrs. It can be used for mending, re-modelling, improving and personalising your things. When sugru cures, it’s flexible rather than rigid. Which means that you can repair things that need to be able to move like textiles, cables, or shoes. Sugru is designed to stick to as many other materials as possible. It forms a strong bond to aluminium, steel, ceramics, glass and other materials including plastics like perspex.

 

 

 

Bedside Pocket – EUR 20

 

Are you sick of having a cluttered mess on your bedside locker? Books, glasses, phone, chargers, all this things on your tiny locker top? Well here’s the answer. A very handy bedside pocket to store all the things you need right beside you while you sleep. Except this way they are kept neat and tidy and out of sight, Made from felt, the pocket has a flap that fits under your mattress to keep it secure.
